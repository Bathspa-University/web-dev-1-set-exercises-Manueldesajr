Marble Cake

flour white sugar milk eggs butter baking powder vanilla salt cocoa powder
2 cups all-purpose flour 1 cup white sugar 1 cup of milk 2 large eggs one half of cup of butter, softened 2 teaspoons baking powder 1 teaspoons vanilla extract one half of teaspoon salt 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).Grease and flour a 9-inch round pan.

Step 2: Place flour, sugar, milk, eggs, butter, baking powder, vanilla and salt into a large bowl.

Step 3: Mix slwoly to moisten, the beat with an eletric mixer at medium speed for about 2 minutes until smooth.

Step 4: Reserve 3/4 cup batter;pour the remainer batter into the prepared pan.

Step 5: Stir cocoa into reserved batter until even in color.

Step 6: Drop chocolate batter by spoonfuls on top of vanilla batter;swirl cocoa batter into white batter using a knife to create a marble appearance.

Step 7: Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean,about 30 to 35 minutes. Cool briefly on a wire rack before inverting carefullyy onto a serving plate or cooling rack. Let cool completely.
