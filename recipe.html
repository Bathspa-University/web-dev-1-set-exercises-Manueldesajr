Ingredients

  • 1lb (454g) wild-caugth shirmp
  • 2 bunches collard greens
  • 2 - 13.5oz (398ml) cans of organic coconut milk
  • 1lb (454g) peanuts (i used roasted unsalted)
  • 4 cloves,pressed
  • 3 tsp (12g) coarsed Celtic sea salt (to taste)
  • 4 (946ml) cups water

Isntructions

  1. Peel and devein shrimp.Bring shells and 2 cups (473ml) of water to boil in a large saucepan for 5 minutes,strain and reserve the liquid
  2. Meanwhile, cut tough ribs of collard greens and discard.Roughly chop leafy parts of the collards blend food processor until pulverized
  3. Dump greens into a large Dutch over,add reserved shirmp shell liquid, 1 tsp (4g) of salt, and 1 can of coconut milk. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 minutes
  4. In a separate saucepan bring 2 cups (473ml) of water to a boil with 1 tsp (4g) of salt. Add shrimp and cook for 5 minutes. Strain and refrigerate shrimp. Return the liquid to saucepan.
  5. Grind peanuts in food processor until powder. Add to saucepan the shrimp broth and 1 can of coconut milk. Heat swoly over medium until boling. Once boilng, add to Dutch oven with the greens. Add 4 cloves of pressed garlic. Add 1 tsp (4g) salt to taste. Simmer for 90 minutes During last 5-10 minutes, add shrimp back to heat throuhgh. served over rice or straight up
